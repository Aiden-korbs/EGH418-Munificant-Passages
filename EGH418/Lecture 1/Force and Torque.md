### Vectors:

![[Screenshot 2025-07-29 at 7.49.02 pm.png|500]]
A force has:
- **Magnitude**
- **Direction**
- **Point of application**

A force can:
- Translate an object
- Rotate an object
- Deform an object
### Vector Resolution:
**X-component:**

$$
F_x = F \cos(\theta)
$$
**Y-component:**

$$
F_y = F \sin(\theta)
$$
