## Components:
- **Effort force** \( F \)
- **Resistance force** \( R \)
- **Axis of rotation**

## Mechanical Advantage:

$$
MA = \frac{\text{Effort arm}}{\text{Resistance arm}}
$$

## Lever Classes:
1. **1st Class**: MA varies (e.g., seesaw)
2. **2nd Class**: MA > 1 (less effort needed)
3. **3rd Class**: MA < 1 (greater speed/range, most common in the human body)
